Camp Kesem







Camp Kesem is a week-long summer camp for children whose parents have been affected by cancer.
As counselors, we fundraise all year to send of our campers to Kesem for free!
At UCLA we have two weeks of camp in order to accomodate our campers.
Camp Week 1: June 24-June30
Camp Week 2: July 3-July 9
